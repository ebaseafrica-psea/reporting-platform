What Is Sexual Exploitation, Abuse, and Harassment?

Sexual Exploitation is using power or vulnerability for personal gain in sexual contexts. Examples include suggesting personal favors for project benefits.

Sexual Abuse includes any non-consensual sexual behavior, especially involving power imbalances or minors.

Sexual Harassment refers to unwelcome behavior like jokes, advances, or touching, making others uncomfortable.
